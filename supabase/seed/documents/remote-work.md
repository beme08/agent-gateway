# Acme Corp Remote Work Policy (Effective 2026-01-01)

## Overview

Acme Corp supports flexible work arrangements. Employees may work remotely on a part-time or full-time basis, subject to manager approval and the rules below.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probation period are eligible to request remote work.

## Short-term remote work (under 30 days)

For short trips, employees may work from another location for up to 30 calendar days per year without additional approval beyond their direct manager. Notify your manager at least 3 business days in advance.

## Long-term remote work (30+ days, including international)

Working remotely from another country for an extended period requires:

1. Written approval from your direct manager.
2. Notification to HR and Legal at least 30 days in advance.
3. Confirmation that your home-country tax and employment-law obligations are met. HR will issue a written summary of obligations.
4. A valid work permit or visa for the destination country. Acme does not sponsor short-term tourist visas for remote work.
5. Continued coverage under Acme's liability and cybersecurity policies.

## Working from a country with sanctions or export-control restrictions

Requests to work from any country subject to U.S. sanctions, OFAC restrictions, or export-control limitations will be declined. Contact Legal for the current list.

## Equipment and security

- Use Acme-issued devices only.
- Connect to the Acme VPN before accessing any company resource.
- Do not work from public, unsecured Wi-Fi without the VPN.
- Paper documents must be stored out of sight and shredded when no longer needed.
