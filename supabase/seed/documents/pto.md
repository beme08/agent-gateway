# Acme Corp Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy (Effective 2026-01-01)

## Overview

Acme Corp provides paid time off (vacation) to all full-time employees. Vacation is intended for rest, personal time, and travel.

## Annual allocation

| Years of service | Vacation days / year |
|------------------|----------------------|
| 0 - 2 years      | 20                   |
| 3 - 5 years      | 25                   |
| 6+ years         | 30                   |

## Accrual

Vacation is accrued monthly. New hires begin accruing from their start date. The maximum accrued balance is 1.5x your annual allocation — once you reach the cap, accrual pauses until you use days.

## How to request PTO

1. Submit a leave request through the HR portal.
2. Your manager will review and approve. Most requests are approved within 2 business days.
3. For requests of 5 or more consecutive days, give your manager at least 14 days notice when possible.

## Approval

Managers are expected to approve PTO unless there is a documented business reason not to. If a request is declined, your manager must propose alternative dates.

## Rolling over

Up to 5 unused vacation days may be rolled into the next calendar year. Anything above 5 is forfeited.

## Selling back

You may sell back up to 5 unused vacation days each year, paid out at your current base rate. The sell-back window opens in November each year.
