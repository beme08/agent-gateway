# Acme Corp Executive Compensation Policy (Confidential — Executive Access Only)

## Overview

This document is confidential and restricted to members of the executive team and the board of directors. It describes the structure of executive compensation at Acme Corp.

## Components

Executive compensation consists of four components:

1. **Base salary** — set by the Compensation Committee annually.
2. **Annual cash bonus** — target 50% of base for the C-suite, 35% for VPs. Tied to company and individual performance.
3. **Equity grants** — Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.
4. **Long-term incentive plan (LTIP)** — performance-share units tied to 3-year TSR relative to a defined peer group.

## Clawback

In the event of a restatement of financial results caused by misconduct, the Compensation Committee may claw back both cash bonuses and unvested equity.

## Severance

Executives terminated without cause receive 12 months of base salary plus accelerated vesting of 50% of unvested time-based equity. Performance-based equity follows the plan document.

## Change of control

In a change-of-control event, unvested time-based equity vests in full. Performance-based equity vests at target unless the acquirer assumes or substitutes the awards.
