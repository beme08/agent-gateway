# Acme Corp Sick Leave Policy (Effective 2026-01-01)

## Overview

Acme Corp provides paid sick leave to all full-time employees. Sick leave is intended for your own illness, medical appointments, or the care of an immediate family member.

## Annual allocation

Every full-time employee receives **10 paid sick days** per calendar year, prorated based on your start date. Unused sick days do not roll over.

## How to request sick leave

If you know in advance (e.g. for a planned medical procedure):

1. Submit a leave request through the HR portal at least 3 business days in advance.
2. Your manager will review and approve.

If you are sick and cannot work that day:

1. Notify your manager as early as possible — a short message on Slack or email is fine.
2. Submit the leave request the same day; it will be approved automatically for sick leave under 3 consecutive days.
3. For sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.

## Doctor's notes

For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, submit a doctor's note to HR within 5 business days of your return.

## Sick leave vs. vacation

Sick leave is separate from vacation and is not deducted from your vacation balance. You cannot use sick leave for non-medical reasons.

## Returning to work

If you have been out for 5 or more consecutive days for a serious health condition, HR may ask for a fitness-for-duty certification before you return.
